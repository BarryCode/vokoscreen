Expected publication vokoscreen 2.6.0 Summer / Autumn 2018

The camera module has been completely redesigned in Qt and is compatible with the future vokoscreen for Windows version.
To do:
- Last used camera is not saved.
- Menu item "Show via full screen" is not saved.


The area display was completely rewritten but not yet built into vokoscreen and works under Windows, Linux as well as under Wayland and XWayland.
To do:
- Install in vokoscreen
- Restrictions result that the area display eg under Gnome can not be pulled over the menu located at the top and left of the screen.
Even if no solution is found, the new area display will appear in vokoscreen 2.6.0

New features in current beta:
- New camera module
- Icons for video players
- Standard player is now determined
- Icons for video formats
- Audiocodec flac, opus
- format webm, mov
- Video codec x265, VP8
- Cache moved from / tmp to the video folder
- And many other things
